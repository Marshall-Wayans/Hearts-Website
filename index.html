Welcome To Hearts Of Care Agency

Hearts of Care Agency is a Domiciliary Care Agency providing live-in carers and care services to adults, allowing them to live safely and independently in their own homes. Live-in home care can provide the practical and emotional support needed to help your loved one stay healthy and happy in the home they love. We understand that choosing care can be difficult, and that is why we are here to help you make the process as smooth as possible.

Are you, or someone close to you, in need of a bit of extra support at home? Hearts of Care Agency is a home care agency that tailors its flexible service to the needs of each individual client. Duties can include preparing meals, assisting with shopping, providing companionship, administering medication and accompanying you or your loved one to appointments. Even if you don’t know exactly what tasks you need help with, you know that you want your care delivered with respect, dignity and compassion.

If you have used a care company before, then you will know that it’s more than just the delivery of care that’s important. It’s also about how the service is managed. At Hearts of Care we put the well-being and quality of life of our clients at the heart of what we do. Our company is based on traditional nurse-led values. We hire only the best care staff, pay them properly and give them the time and resources they need to do their job well.
